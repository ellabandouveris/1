Ella Roze Bandouveris (b. 2001, Los Angeles) is a multimedia artist and designer interested in sculptural objects. Medium agnostic, her work illustrates sunsets, car interiors, palm trees, sidewalks, and shadows, meditating on her lived experience as the oldest of three sisters, born and raised in Los Angeles. Guided by visual familiarity, her work aims to reflect on the familiar, and also reinvent design futures.

Bandouveris has just finished pursuing a B.A. in Design | Media Art at UCLA and graduated in June of 2023.
