She Peels The Orange And Always Gives Me Half

February 2023.
Frame: Reclaimed Wood.
Cushion: Linen, Denim.

This piece is a collaboration between Sisters, Ella and Tessa Bandouveris. Designed together. Frame fabricated in Los Angeles, California by Ella Bandouveris. Cushion fabricated in Berkeley, California by Tessa Bandouveris.

Rooted in the materiality that forms the structure, we used found and reclaimed wood. Us sisters have always had a particular interest in furniture design and sculptural functionality, we are excited to be at the beginning of many more creative endeavors.
