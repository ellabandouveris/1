I Am Older Now

Created in December 2021 using p5js.

This non-linear interactive work explores the relationship I have with the two homes I grew up in. I created two abstract representations of what it means to live at my mom's house and live at my dad's house.

The looping algorithm that takes the user back to the pink envelope addressed to 'Ella' represents the way that these two places have become contents within my indentity, just as they are contents of the evelope.
