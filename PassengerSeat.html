I Looked Over At You In My Passenger Seat

Created in January 2022 using p5js.

A generative collage, referencing archive images by Ella Roze Bandouveris. Using the car interior as a frame, each random generated image shuffles and index of foreground, middleground, and background "tagged" images creating a new composition every time.
