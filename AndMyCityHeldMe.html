And My City Held Me

Multi-part project, July 2022 - May 2023.
And My City Held Me is a photo book that reveals a perspective of contemporary girlhood within the context of the City of Los Angeles.

This sustained investigation has helped me to process growing up in LA. I have been able to visit "spots" that informed and inspired me as a kid. I have been able to commemorate these places as well. Since releasing the book and film, young people have reached out to me sharing that that they were able to relate to the essence of LA youth. I aimed to capture nostalgia while documenting pure the Los Angeles landscape, friendship love, and youth culture.
